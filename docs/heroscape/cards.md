> Canonical Army Card roster, summarized for adaptation from the user's card scans

## Edition note

- This PDF is a **rebalanced modern printing**, NOT the classic 2004 Rise of the
  Valkyrie set. Known deviations from classic values:
  - **Marro Warriors** — 105 points / Range 6 (classic: 50 points / Range 8).
  - **Raelin the Kyrie Warrior** — 125 points (classic: 80). Figure **Range is 1
    in BOTH editions**: an earlier note here said "classic Range 4", but the
    user-supplied RotV card (Index_3x5 …-ROTV.pdf) shows Range 1 — the "4" is her
    aura's REACH, not a Range stat. Engine: HS_CARDS raelin 125, CLASSIC_OVERRIDES 80.
  - **Izumi Samurai** — Attack 2. (A prior "classic Attack 3" claim here is
    UNVERIFIED against a card, so it is NOT applied — Classic mode is points-only.)
  - **Raelin's DEFENSIVE AURA (RotV card)** — within **4** clear-sight spaces,
    **+2** defense dice, excludes Raelin. An earlier scan said "Extended Defensive
    Aura" 6 / +1 (plus a bogus Whirlwind Assault) — both wrong, corrected to the card.
- All six squads print as **UNIQUE SQUAD** in this edition (recorded as printed).
- **POLICY: cards win for card content** (stats, power names, power text); **the
  rulebook wins for core rules** (turn structure, range counting, line of sight,
  special-attack handling).
- Cards are **single-sided** in this printing — there is no separate Basic side.
  The Basic Game slice uses these same stats and ignores special powers.
- Squad figure counts are not printed as a stat on any card; they come from the
  rulebook (Airborne Elite's count of 4 is additionally confirmed on-card inside
  The Drop's text).

## Card details

Power text below is the faithful printed rules text from the extraction files;
italic notes preserve the extractions' mechanical clarifications and

### Jandar

#### Tarn Viking Warriors — 50 points

Unique Squad (4 figures) · Warriors · Human · Wild · Earth · Medium 5
Life 1 · Move 4 · Range 1 · Attack 3 · Defense 4

**BERSERKER CHARGE** — After moving and before attacking, roll the 20-sided
die. If you roll a 15 or higher, you may move all Tarn Viking Warriors again.
*Timing window is strictly after the squad's move and before its attack; one
d20 roll; threshold 15+; the extra move is optional ("may") and applies to ALL
Tarn Viking Warriors, not just one figure. No printed limit on repeats is
visible in the card text.*

#### Finn the Viking Champion — 80 points

Unique Hero · Champion · Human · Valiant · Earth · Medium 5
Life 4 · Move 5 · Range 1 · Attack 3 · Defense 4

**ATTACK AURA 1** — All friendly figures adjacent to Finn with a range of 1
add 1 die to their normal attack.
*Unconditional aura; restricted to friendly figures that BOTH are adjacent to
Finn AND have a printed Range of 1; grants exactly 1 additional die on NORMAL
attacks only — not special attacks.*

**WARRIOR'S ATTACK SPIRIT 1** — When destroyed, place this figure on any
unique Army Card. Adds 1 to the normal attack number on that card.
*Wording verified at high res: unlike Thorgrim's card, the printed text does
NOT name Finn in either sentence. Trigger on Finn's destruction; place on any
unique Army Card of the owner's choice; +1 to that card's NORMAL Attack
number.*

#### Thorgrim the Viking Champion — 80 points

Unique Hero · Champion · Human · Valiant · Earth · Medium 5
Life 4 · Move 5 · Range 1 · Attack 3 · Defense 4

**DEFENSIVE AURA 1** — All friendly figures adjacent to Thorgrim add 1 die to
their defense.
*Unconditional aura — no roll, no "may"; applies to ALL friendly figures while
adjacent to Thorgrim; grants exactly 1 additional defense die.*

**WARRIOR'S ARMOR SPIRIT 1** — When Thorgrim is destroyed, place this figure
on any unique Army Card. Thorgrim's Spirit adds 1 to the defense number on
that card.
*Trigger: on Thorgrim's destruction; the owner places the figure on a unique
Army Card of their choice; +1 to that card's printed Defense number.*

#### Airborne Elite — 110 points

Unique Squad (4 figures, confirmed on-card by The Drop's text) · Soldiers ·
Human · Disciplined · Earth · Medium 5
Life 1 · Move 4 · Range 8 · Attack 3 · Defense 2

**GRENADE SPECIAL ATTACK** — Range 5. Lob 12. Attack 2.
Use this power once per game. Start the game with a grenade marker on this
card. Remove the grenade marker to throw grenades. One at a time do the
following with each Airborne Elite: Choose a figure to attack. No clear line
of sight is needed. Any figures adjacent to the chosen figure are also
affected by the Grenade Special Attack. Roll 2 attack dice once for all
affected figures. Each figure rolls defense dice separately.
*Matches the rulebook's grenade-lob worked example exactly (attacker rolls the
2 attack dice ONCE for all affected figures; each defender rolls defense
separately). The components list includes exactly one Grenade Marker,
consistent with once-per-game.*

**THE DROP** — Airborne Elite do not start the game on the battlefield. At the
start of each round, before you place Order Markers, roll the 20-sided die.
If you roll a 13 or higher you may place all 4 Airborne Elite figures on any
empty spaces. You cannot place them adjacent to each other or other figures,
or on glyphs.
*Verified at high res: the final sentence contains NO "any" — it reads exactly
"You cannot place them adjacent to each other or other figures, or on
glyphs." — and the card prints no comma after "13 or higher". The Drop is
named in the rulebook's Simultaneous Special Powers section.*

#### Sgt. Drake Alexander — 110 points

Unique Hero · Soldier · Human · Valiant · Earth · Medium 5
Life 5 · Move 5 · Range 1 · Attack 6 · Defense 3

**THORIAN SPEED** — Opponents' figures must be adjacent to Sgt. Drake
Alexander to attack him with a normal attack.
*A restriction on OPPONENTS, and only on NORMAL attacks — non-adjacent normal
(ranged) attacks cannot target him; special attacks are not restricted.*

**GRAPPLE GUN 25** — Instead of Sgt. Drake Alexander's normal move, he may
move only one space. This space may be up to 25 levels higher. When using the
Grapple Gun, all engagement rules still apply.
*Replacement move ("Instead of … normal move"), permission "may", exactly one
space, up to 25 levels higher; engagement rules — including
leaving-engagement swipes — explicitly still apply.*

#### Raelin the Kyrie Warrior — 125 points (RotV card)

Unique Hero · Warrior · Kyrie · Merciful · Valhalla · Medium 5
Life 5 · Move 6 · Range 1 · Attack 3 · Defense 3
*Authoritative source: the user-supplied RotV index card
(Index_3x5_Raelin_The__Kyrie_Warrior-ROTV.pdf). Range is 1 (the "4" some notes
cite is her aura's REACH, not a Range stat). Points 125; Classic edition = 80.*

**DEFENSIVE AURA** — All figures you control within 4 clear sight spaces of
Raelin add 2 to their defense dice. Raelin's Defensive Aura does not affect Raelin.
*Scope: figures YOU CONTROL. Radius: 4 clear sight spaces. Bonus: +2 defense
dice. Explicit self-exclusion. (Supersedes an earlier scan that recorded an
"Extended Defensive Aura" of 6 spaces / +1 die plus a Whirlwind Assault — both
were from a different/incorrect card and have been removed.) The rulebook's
clear-sight-spaces measurement (from the source figure's Target Point; any
visible part of the figure counts) applies.*

**FLYING** — When counting spaces for Raelin's movement, ignore elevations.
Raelin may fly over water without stopping, pass over figures without becoming
engaged, and fly over obstacles such as ruins. When Raelin starts to fly, if
she is engaged she will take any leaving engagement attacks.
*Elevation changes cost nothing; water/figures/obstacles are overflown;
takeoff is not free — leaving-engagement attacks DO apply when she starts to
fly while engaged (matches the rulebook's flying example).*

### Utgar

#### Zettian Guards — 70 points

Unique Squad (2 figures) · Guards · Soulborg · Precise · Alpha Prime · Medium 5
Life 1 · Move 4 · Range 7 · Attack 2 · Defense 7

**ZETTIAN TARGETING** — When attacking, if your second Zettian Guard attacks
the same figure as the first Zettian Guard, add one attack die to the second
Zettian Guard's attack.
*Trigger: "When attacking". Condition: the SECOND Guard attacks the SAME
figure the FIRST Guard attacked this turn. Effect: +1 attack die to the second
Guard's attack only. Imperative "add" — not optional.*

#### Ne-Gok-Sa — 90 points

Unique Hero · Warlord · Marro · Tricky · Marr · Medium 5
Life 5 · Move 5 · Range 1 · Attack 3 · Defense 6

**MIND SHACKLE 20** — After moving and before attacking, you may choose any
unique figure adjacent to Ne-gok-sa. Roll the 20-sided die. If you roll a 20,
take control of the chosen figure and that figure's Army Card. You now control
that Army Card and all figures on it. Remove any Order Markers on this card.
If Ne-Gok-Sa is destroyed, you retain control of any previously Mind Shackled
Army Cards.
*Trigger window is exactly "After moving and before attacking"; the choice is
optional ("may"); target must be a unique figure adjacent to Ne-Gok-Sa;
success only on a natural 20; control transfer includes the Army Card and ALL
figures on it; Order Markers on the shackled card are removed; control
persists even if Ne-Gok-Sa is later destroyed.*

#### Marro Warriors — 105 points

Unique Squad (4 figures) · Warriors · Marro · Wild · Marr · Medium 4
Life 1 · Move 6 · Range 6 · Attack 2 · Defense 3
*Points 105 and Range 6 are as printed (verified at high res). Both deviate
from the classic 2004 RotV values (50 points, Range 8), consistent with this
PDF being a later/rebalanced printing.*

**WATER CLONE** — Instead of attacking with the Marro Warriors, roll the
20-sided die for each Marro Warrior in play. If you roll a 15 or higher, place
a previously destroyed Marro Warrior on a same-level space adjacent to that
Marro Warrior. Any Marro Warrior on a water space needs a 10 or higher to
Water Clone. You may only Water Clone after you move.
*Cloning replaces the attack ("Instead of attacking"); one d20 roll per Marro
Warrior currently in play; threshold 15+ normally, 10+ for a Warrior standing
on a water space; the returned figure must be a previously destroyed Marro
Warrior; placement must be on a same-level space adjacent to the Warrior that
rolled; timing restriction is exactly "You may only Water Clone after you
move."*

#### Deathwalker 9000 — 140 points

Unique Hero · Deathwalker · Soulborg · Precise · Alpha Prime · Large 7
Life 1 · Move 5 · Range 7 · Attack 4 · Defense 7

**EXPLOSION SPECIAL ATTACK** — Range 7. Attack 3. Choose a figure to attack.
Any figures adjacent to the chosen figure are also affected by the Explosion
Special Attack. Deathwalker only needs a clear sight shot at the chosen
figure. Roll 3 attack dice once for all affected figures. Each figure rolls
defense dice separately. Deathwalker can be affected by his own Explosion
Special Attack.
*Special Attack — per the rulebook it can never be modified by glyphs, other
powers, or height advantage. LOS ("clear sight shot") is required only to the
CHOSEN figure; figures adjacent to the chosen figure are hit too — friend or
foe, no "enemy" qualifier. The 3 attack dice are rolled ONCE for all affected
figures; each defender rolls defense separately. Deathwalker himself CAN be
affected by his own explosion.*

**RANGE ENHANCEMENT** — Any Soulborg Guards adjacent to Deathwalker add 2
spaces to their range.
*Condition: Soulborg species + Guards class, adjacent to Deathwalker. The
Zettian Guards qualify: Range 7 → 9 while adjacent. Imperative "add" — always
on while adjacent.*

#### Mimring — 150 points

Unique Hero · Beast · Dragon · Ferocious · Icaria · Huge 9
Life 5 · Move 6 · Range 1 · Attack 4 · Defense 3

**FIRE LINE SPECIAL ATTACK** — Range Special. Attack 4. Choose 8 spaces in a
straight line from Mimring. All figures on those spaces who are in line of
sight are affected by Mimring's Fire Line Special Attack. Roll 4 attack dice
once for all affected figures. Affected figures roll defense dice separately.
*Numbers: 8 spaces, straight line, 4 attack dice rolled ONCE for everyone;
each affected figure rolls its own defense separately. Only figures in line of
sight on those spaces are affected.*

**FLYING** — When counting spaces for Mimring's movement, ignore elevations.
Mimring may fly over water without stopping, pass over figures without
becoming engaged, and fly over obstacles such as ruins. When Mimring starts to
fly, if he is engaged he will take any leaving engagement attacks.
*Elevation changes cost nothing; water/figures/obstacles are overflown;
leaving-engagement attacks DO apply when he starts to fly while engaged.*

#### Grimnak — 160 points

Unique Hero · Champion · Orc · Ferocious · Grut · Huge 11
Life 5 · Move 5 · Range 1 · Attack 2 · Defense 4

**CHOMP** — Before attacking, choose one medium or small figure adjacent to
Grimnak. If the chosen figure is a Squad figure, destroy it. If the chosen
figure is a Hero figure, roll the 20-sided die. If you roll a 16 or higher,
destroy the chosen Hero.
*Unconditional auto-destroy on Squad figures (no roll); Hero destroy requires
a d20 roll of 16+. Trigger window is "Before attacking". Target restriction:
one medium or small figure adjacent to Grimnak — large/huge figures cannot be
Chomped.*

**ORC WARRIOR ENHANCEMENT** — All friendly Orc Warriors adjacent to Grimnak
roll an additional attack die and an additional defense die.

### Einar

#### Izumi Samurai — 60 points

Unique Squad (3 figures) · Samurai · Human · Disciplined · Earth · Medium 5
Life 1 · Move 6 · Range 1 · Attack 2 · Defense 5
*Attack 2 is as printed (corrected at high res from an earlier read of 3).
External-knowledge caveat from the extraction, NOT from the scan: official
printings list Izumi Attack as 3, so this PDF deviates here — recorded as
printed.*

**COUNTER STRIKE** — When rolling defense dice against a normal attack from an
adjacent attacking figure, all excess shields count as unblockable hits on the
attacking figure. This power does not work against other Samurai.
*Applies only when DEFENDING, only against a NORMAL attack (not special
attacks), only from an ADJACENT attacking figure; excess shields (shields
beyond the skulls rolled) become unblockable hits on the attacker; explicitly
does not work against other Samurai.*

## Engine notes

- **PendingChoice (player decisions — never auto-resolve):**
  - *Mind Shackle* — optional; choose any adjacent unique figure, exactly in
    the after-move/before-attack window.
  - *Chomp* — choose one adjacent medium-or-small figure before attacking.
  - *Grenade Special Attack* — per-Elite target choice, resolved one Elite at
    a time; splash automatically hits all figures adjacent to each chosen
    target (no LOS needed).
  - *The Drop* — placement choices for all 4 figures on empty spaces,
    validating: not adjacent to each other or to other figures, not on glyphs.
  - *Water Clone* — decide to clone instead of attacking (only after moving);
    choose placement of each returned Warrior on a same-level adjacent space.
  - *Spirit placements on destroy* (Thorgrim's Warrior's Armor Spirit, Finn's
    Warrior's Attack Spirit) — owner chooses any unique Army Card; this
    interrupts whoever's turn caused the destruction.
  - Other optional "may" decisions to surface: Berserker Charge re-move,
    Syvarris's Double Attack second attack, Whirlwind Assault target set,
    Grapple Gun replacement move, Fire Line line selection.
- **Passive auras — recompute on adjacency/range change:** Thorgrim's
  Defensive Aura 1 (+1 defense, adjacency), Finn's Attack Aura 1 (+1 normal
  attack, adjacency + printed Range 1), Raelin's Extended Defensive Aura
  (+1 defense, 6 clear-sight spaces, figures you control, excludes Raelin —
  recompute on any movement/LOS change, not just adjacency). Same bucket for
  adjacency-based stat enhancements: Orc Warrior Enhancement (+1 attack die /
  +1 defense die for friendly Orc Warriors adjacent to Grimnak) and Range
  Enhancement (+2 Range for Soulborg Guards adjacent to Deathwalker 9000;
  Zettian Guards 7 → 9).
- **Once-per-game:** Grenade Special Attack (grenade marker starts on the
  card; remove it to use). The Drop's placement resolves at most once, but its
  d20 roll recurs at the start of every round (before Order Markers) until it
  succeeds.
- **d20 powers and thresholds (as printed):**
  - Berserker Charge — 15+ (may move all Tarn Viking Warriors again; no
    printed repeat limit).
  - Water Clone — 15+ per Marro Warrior in play; 10+ for a Warrior on a water
    space.
  - Mind Shackle — success only on a 20.
  - Chomp — 16+ vs Heroes; Squad figures are destroyed automatically, no roll.
  - The Drop — 13+, rolled at the start of each round before Order Markers.
- **Cross-references:** special attacks (Fire Line, Explosion, Grenade) follow
  [04-combat-range-los-attack.md](04-combat-range-los-attack.md) — never
  modified by glyphs, other powers, or height advantage; one attack roll for
  all affected figures, separate defense rolls. Simultaneous-power roll-offs
  (e.g. both players' Drops at the same round start) follow
  [05-glyphs-special-powers.md](05-glyphs-special-powers.md), which also has
  the grenade-lob worked example. Counter Strike, Stealth Dodge, Thorian
  Speed, and Finn's Attack Aura all hinge on the normal-vs-special attack
  distinction — Counter Strike, Thorian Speed, and Attack Aura 1 apply to
  normal attacks only.
