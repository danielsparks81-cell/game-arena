## Raelin the Kyrie Warrior
- General: Jandar | Species: Kyrie | Uniqueness: Unique Hero | Class: Warrior | Personality: Resolute | Homeworld: Valhalla
- Size/Height: Medium 5 | Life: 5 (verified at high res) | Move: 6 | Range: 1 (verified at high res — at native crop resolution, ~55 px tall, the glyph is unambiguously a "1" with a top-left serif flag; it is NOT a "4" with a lost crossbar — compare the clearly different "4" glyphs on Zettian Move and Grimnak Defense in the same font. - ⚠ CROSS-CHECK MISMATCH (rulebook vs card — recording card as printed, not correcting): the
  rulebook's worked examples (extraction/pages-15-21.md) describe Raelin's aura as
  "**Defensive Aura**", radius **4** clear-sight spaces, granting "**+2 defense dice**". This card
  prints "**Extended Defensive Aura**", radius **6** clear sight spaces, granting **+1** defense
  die. Name, radius, and bonus all differ. The rulebook's clear-sight-spaces measurement rule
  (from the source figure's Target Point; any visible part of the figure counts) still applies.

### Powers
#### WHIRLWIND ASSAULT
"Raelin may attack any or all figures adjacent to her. Roll each attack separately."
(Permission "may". Targets: any or all **adjacent** figures — card does not say "enemy figures",
it says "figures". Each attack is rolled **separately**.)

#### EXTENDED DEFENSIVE AURA
"All figures you control within 6 clear sight spaces of Raelin add 1 to their defense dice.
Raelin's Extended Defensive Aura does not affect Raelin."
(Scope: figures **you control** — not all friendly-player figures. Radius: **6 clear sight
spaces**. Bonus: **add 1** defense die. Explicit self-exclusion: does **not** affect Raelin.)

#### FLYING
"When counting spaces for Raelin's movement, ignore elevations. Raelin may fly over water without
stopping, pass over figures without becoming engaged, and fly over obstacles such as ruins. When
Raelin starts to fly, if she is engaged she will take any leaving engagement attacks."
(Ignore elevations when counting move spaces. "May" fly over water **without stopping**; pass
over figures **without becoming engaged**; over obstacles such as ruins. Takeoff clause: "when
Raelin starts to fly, if she is engaged" → she **will take** any leaving engagement attacks.
Matches the rulebook p. 16 flying example — takeoff is not free.)

## Deathwalker 9000
- General: Utgar | Species: Soulborg | Uniqueness: Unique Hero | Class: Deathwalker | Personality: Precise | Homeworld: Alpha Prime
- Size/Height: Large 7 | Life: 1 | Move: 5 | Range: 7 | Attack: 4 | Defense: 7 | Points: 140
- ✔ CROSS-CHECK: Height **7** matches the rulebook adjacency examples (Finn Height 5 vs
  Deathwalker Height 7, ruin height 6).

### Powers
#### EXPLOSION SPECIAL ATTACK
"Range 7. Attack 3. Choose a figure to attack. Any figures adjacent to the chosen figure are also
affected by the Explosion Special Attack. Deathwalker only needs a clear sight shot at the chosen
figure. Roll 3 attack dice once for all affected figures. Each figure rolls defense dice
separately. Deathwalker can be affected by his own Explosion Special Attack."
(Special Attack — per rulebook p. 15 it can **never** be modified by glyphs, other powers, or
height advantage. Range **7**, Attack **3**. LOS ("clear sight shot") required only to the
**chosen** figure; figures **adjacent to the chosen figure** are hit too — friend or foe, no
"enemy" qualifier. Roll the **3** attack dice **once** for all affected figures; each defender
rolls defense **separately**. Deathwalker himself **can** be affected by his own explosion.)

#### RANGE ENHANCEMENT
"Any Soulborg Guards adjacent to Deathwalker add 2 spaces to their range."
(Condition: **Soulborg** species + **Guards** class, **adjacent** to Deathwalker. Effect: add
**2** spaces to Range. The Zettian Guards on this page are Soulborg/Guards → they qualify:
Range 7 → 9 while adjacent. Imperative "add" — always on while adjacent.)
