## Ne-Gok-Sa
- General: Utgar | Species: Marro | Uniqueness: UNIQUE HERO | Class: Warlord | Personality: Tricky | Homeworld: Marr
- Size/Height: MEDIUM 5 | Life: 5 | Move: 5 | Range: 1 | Attack: 3 | Defense: 6 | Points: 90

Defense: 6 (verified at high res — single-lobe glyph with open top confirmed on the p2-TL crop; an 8 would show two stacked closed bowls as in the "80" points print on the Viking champions. Not 8.)

### Powers
#### MIND SHACKLE 20
After moving and before attacking, you may choose any unique figure adjacent to Ne-gok-sa. Roll the 20-sided die. If you roll a 20, take control of the chosen figure and that figure's Army Card. You now control that Army Card and all figures on it. Remove any Order Markers on this card. If Ne-Gok-Sa is destroyed, you retain control of any previously Mind Shackled Army Cards.

(Trigger window is exactly "After moving and before attacking"; the choice is optional ("may"); target must be a unique figure adjacent to Ne-Gok-Sa; success only on a natural 20 on the 20-sided die; control transfer includes the Army Card and ALL figures on it; Order Markers on the shackled card are removed; control persists even if Ne-Gok-Sa is later destroyed.)

## Marro Warriors
- General: Utgar | Species: Marro | Uniqueness: UNIQUE SQUAD | Class: Warriors | Personality: Wild | Homeworld: Marr
- Size/Height: MEDIUM 4 | Life: 1 | Move: 6 (verified at high res) | Range: 6 (corrected at high res; low-res pass read 8 — on the p2-TR crop the Range glyph is identical to the single-lobe Move "6" above it and to Ne-Gok-Sa's Defense "6", not a double-bowl 8) | Attack: 2 | Defense: 3 | Points: 105 (corrected at high res; low-res pass read 50 — the crop clearly prints three digits, "105")

Points: 105 (verified at high res — the p2-TR crop unambiguously prints three digits "105"; the earlier 50 reading was wrong. Note this deviates from the classic 2004 RotV cost of 50, consistent with this PDF being a later/rebalanced printing.)
Move: 6 / Range: 6 (verified at high res — both glyphs are identical single-lobe 6s on the crop; the earlier "Range 8, double lobe" reading was wrong. Reference 8s with two closed bowls appear in the Viking champions' "80" points print and look clearly different.)
⚠ Squad figure count: not printed as a number. The name-banner silhouette cluster appears to show **4** figures (matches the expected Marro squad count of 4), but the icons are too small to count with certainty.

### Powers
#### WATER CLONE
Instead of attacking with the Marro Warriors, roll the 20-sided die for each Marro Warrior in play. If you roll a 15 or higher, place a previously destroyed Marro Warrior on a same-level space adjacent to that Marro Warrior. Any Marro Warrior on a water space needs a 10 or higher to Water Clone. You may only Water Clone after you move.

(Cloning replaces the attack — "Instead of attacking"; one d20 roll per Marro Warrior currently in play; threshold 15+ normally, reduced to 10+ for a Warrior standing on a water space; the returned figure must be a previously destroyed Marro Warrior; placement must be on a same-level space adjacent to the Warrior that rolled; timing restriction is exactly "You may only Water Clone after you move.")

## Agent Carr
- General: Vydar | Species: Human | Uniqueness: UNIQUE HERO | Class: Agent | Personality: Tricky | Homeworld: Earth
- Size/Height: MEDIUM 5 | Life: 4 | Move: 5 | Range: 6 | Attack: 2 | Defense: 4 | Points: 100

Cross-check: matches the rulebook anchor exactly (Life 4, Move 5, Range 6, Attack 2, Defense 4, 100 points). No mismatch.

### Powers
#### GHOST WALK
Agent Carr can move through all figures.

#### SWORD OF RECKONING 4
If Agent Carr is attacking an adjacent figure, add 4 dice to Agent Carr's attack.

(Conditional bonus: applies only when the attack target is adjacent; adds exactly 4 attack dice.)

#### DISENGAGE
Agent Carr is never attacked when leaving an engagement.

(Unconditional immunity to leaving-engagement attacks.)
